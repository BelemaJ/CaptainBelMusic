About

Captain BEL is a multidisciplinary artist and storyteller whose work blends music, spoken word, and creative expression into powerful experiences. Refusing to be confined by a single genre, Captain BEL explores a range of sounds and perspectives while creating meaningful connections through authentic storytelling.


From music and performance to songwriting and future work in film and television, Captain BEL's artistic vision is rooted in creativity, imagination, and impact.
